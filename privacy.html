Privacy Policy

Effective July 20, 2026. Plain English, because that's how we do everything.

What we collect

Information you give us: when you request an audit or contact us, we collect what you submit — typically your name, email, phone, market, and message. We use it to respond and to provide our services. We don't sell it.

Information collected automatically: like most websites, we use cookies and similar technologies. Essential cookies make the site work. With your consent, we also use analytics (to understand what content is useful) and advertising pixels (to reach people likely to be interested in our services, e.g. via Meta or Google).

Cookies & your choices

On your first visit we ask whether to enable analytics and advertising cookies. Choose "Essentials only" and no marketing pixels load. You can change your mind anytime via the Cookie preferences link (also in the footer of every page). Your browser's settings can also block or delete cookies entirely.

Third parties

If you consent to marketing cookies, data may be shared with the platforms whose pixels we use (such as Meta and Google) under their own privacy policies. Our contact form submissions are processed by our form/email provider solely to deliver your message to us.

Data retention & your rights

We keep contact submissions as long as needed to serve you and meet legal obligations. You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing hello@citedrealty.com. If you're in a jurisdiction with specific privacy rights (e.g. California, EU/UK), we honor the rights those laws provide.

Contact

Questions about this policy: hello@citedrealty.com.

This policy is a starting template and should be reviewed by your attorney before launch, especially if you serve EU/UK visitors or run remarketing campaigns.
