# Content & Positioning Gap: CitedRealty vs. Luxury Presence

Analyzed 2026-07-20 (luxurypresence.com homepage, /real-estate-websites/, /blog/).

## 1. Who they are

Luxury Presence is the category leader for agent/team websites + marketing: custom-designed
websites (IDX, lead capture), "SEO & GEO" services, social media, paid ads, AI CRM, and
client tools (CMAs, branded app). Claims 20,000+ real estate businesses, $450B transaction
volume, 4.8/5 from 2,500+ reviews. Segments: solo agents, celebrity agents, teams, brokerages.
Pricing is hidden behind a demo call (/plans/ has no numbers).

## 2. Service overlap and where we differentiate

| Area | Luxury Presence | CitedRealty | Our angle |
|---|---|---|---|
| Websites | Custom design, IDX, platform-hosted | **New service page live** | **You own the site outright** — theirs is rented SaaS; churn = lose the site. Schema-first, AI-citable build. |
| SEO/GEO | "SEO & GEO" add-on service | Flagship | We lead with citations; they lead with design. We show *how* (entity, mentions, tracking). |
| GBP | Included on higher tiers | Core service | Parity; we detail spam defense + justifications. |
| Social | Managed service | Guidance → full mgmt by tier | Parity. |
| Content | Blog writing on higher tiers | Core engine w/ TL;DR + FAQ schema | Parity+. |
| CRM / app / CMAs | Big platform suite | Not offered | Don't chase. Position as "we plug into your CRM," not platform lock-in. |
| Pricing | Hidden, demo-gated | **Public $999–$6,999** | Keep public — it's a trust wedge and an AI-answer advantage ("how much does…" queries). |

## 3. Their content moat (what we lack)

Their blog is a citation machine aimed at *agents researching marketing* — same audience as ours:
1. **"Best X companies" listicles** — e.g. "Best Google Ads Management Companies for Realtors (2026)",
   "10 Best Social Media Marketing Companies", "Best Real Estate Digital Marketing Agencies 2026".
   These rank for buyer-intent queries and get cited by AI when agents ask "who should I hire."
2. **Statistics roundups** — "50 Real Estate SEO Statistics 2026", "50 Real Estate AEO Statistics",
   "35 Paid Advertising Statistics", "34 Social Media Statistics". Stats pages are AI-citation magnets.
3. **Comparison category** — vendor-vs-vendor content (bottom funnel).
4. **Story-led posts** — narrative case content ("Five website calls last year. Two closings. That's $8M.").
5. **Formats we don't have:** podcast, webinars, case studies, design portfolio, talent network.
